## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with the following parties:

### 4.1 Merchants and Their Riders
**Important**: Around You operates on a merchant-controlled model where:
- **Merchants** are independent shop owners who control their own operations
- **Riders** are managed and controlled by individual merchants, not by Around You
- **Delivery Zones** are defined and controlled by each merchant for their specific shop

When you place an order, we share the following information with the merchant you order from:
- **Order Details**: Items ordered, quantities, special instructions
- **Delivery Information**: Your delivery address, contact phone number, and delivery preferences
- **Customer Information**: Your name and contact details necessary for order fulfillment

The merchant may then share your delivery information with their designated riders to:
- Complete the delivery to your specified address
- Contact you regarding delivery updates or issues
- Navigate to your delivery location

**Note**: Merchants and their riders are independent entities. We are not responsible for how merchants or their riders handle your information. However, we require merchants to handle your information in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

### 4.2 Service Providers
We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in:
- **Payment Processing**: Secure payment processors to handle transactions
- **Analytics**: Service providers who help us analyze app usage and improve our services
- **Cloud Storage**: Data storage and hosting services
- **Communication Services**: Email and SMS service providers for notifications
- **Location Services**: Mapping and geolocation service providers (e.g., Google Maps)

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

### 4.3 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

### 4.4 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information when required by:
- **Law or Legal Process**: Court orders, subpoenas, or other legal requirements
- **Government Authorities**: Requests from government or regulatory authorities
- **Protection of Rights**: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users, merchants, riders, or others
- **Fraud Prevention**: To prevent, detect, or investigate fraud or security issues

### 4.5 With Your Consent
We may share your information with other parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.
