# Release Notes - AroundYou v1.0.0

## Initial Release - Welcome to AroundYou!

We're excited to introduce **AroundYou**, your local shopping companion that connects you with nearby shops and restaurants in your area.

---

##  What's New

### For Consumers

**Discover Local Shops**
- Find shops near you with our location-based search
- Browse shops by type and see what's available
- View shop details, hours, and delivery information

**Smart Shopping Experience**
- Create carts from multiple shops at once
- Add items to your cart with ease
- Real-time inventory updates

**Easy Ordering**
- Place orders with just a few taps
- Track your orders in real-time from placement to delivery
- Get live updates on order status


**Real-Time Updates**
- Get instant notifications when your order status changes
- Track your order from preparation to delivery
- Stay informed every step of the way

### For Merchants

**Shop Management**
- Create and manage your shop profile
- Set your shop location and delivery areas
- Upload shop images and information

**Inventory Control**
- Manage your product catalog with categories
- Add, edit, and organize items easily
- Track item performance and sales

**Order Management**
- View and manage incoming orders
- Update order status in real-time
- Track order history and analytics

**Delivery Zone Setup**
- Define custom delivery areas on a map
- Set delivery fees based on distance
- Configure minimum order values

**Runner Management**
- Assign delivery runners to orders
- Track delivery assignments
- Manage your delivery team
